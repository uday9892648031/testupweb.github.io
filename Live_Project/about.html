About us
All about our restaurent

The Fusion Kitchen is located in the outskirts of Mumbai with soothing ambience since 1985.
It is a pretty place with awesome decor and great food. In collaboration with working background the kitchen creates modern, seasonal and focused on your pleasure dishes.
We provide you with daily self-made bread, sourdough pizza, roasted fish meat vegetables and many more.
A lot of people all around the world admire food. Big lovers of Indian culinary majesty, pizza, different kinds of pasta, meatballs and spaghetti come whether to Italy or to our restaurant. We do our best to serve you delicious food.
